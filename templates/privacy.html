Privacy Policy

Data Controller

MarkDownConverter

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, you may contact us using the email above.

Description of the Service

This service provides file-to-Markdown conversion using automated processing powered by Microsoft MarkItDown.

Data Processing

Files uploaded by users are processed transiently in memory for the sole purpose of conversion.

  • Files are not stored on disk
  • Files are not saved in databases
  • Files are not retained after processing

Once the conversion is completed, all file data is automatically discarded.

Personal Data

We do not require user accounts, registration, or direct submission of personal data.

However, limited technical data may be processed as part of operating the service, including:

  • IP address
  • Request metadata (e.g., timestamps, request size)

This data is not used to identify users and is not combined with other data sources.

Legal Basis (GDPR)

Processing of uploaded files is based on Article 6(1)(f) GDPR (legitimate interests), specifically to provide the file conversion service requested by the user.

Processing of limited technical data is also based on legitimate interest, for the purposes of ensuring service security, preventing abuse, and maintaining system integrity.

These interests are not overridden by the rights and freedoms of users, as:

  • Files are processed transiently and not stored
  • No profiling or tracking is performed
  • Appropriate technical safeguards are implemented

Infrastructure and Hosting

This service is hosted on Railway.

Railway may process certain technical data, such as IP addresses, at the infrastructure level for security and operational purposes. Such processing is subject to Railway's own privacy practices and retention policies.

Error Logging

Application logs are limited to critical errors.

In rare cases of system errors, limited portions of uploaded file content may be temporarily included in error logs for debugging purposes.

These logs:

  • Are restricted in access
  • Are automatically rotated
  • Are not used for any purpose other than maintaining the service

Analytics

This service may use privacy-friendly analytics (such as Plausible Analytics) to collect aggregated, anonymized usage data.

  • No cookies are used for tracking
  • No personal data is collected for analytics purposes
  • No cross-site tracking is performed

Analytics data is used solely to understand general usage patterns (e.g., number of conversions, file types used).

Data Retention

  • Uploaded files: not stored
  • Application logs: retained for a limited period (e.g., up to 7 days via infrastructure provider)
  • Analytics data: aggregated and anonymized

Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or disclose user data to third parties.

Data may be processed by:

  • Railway (hosting infrastructure)
  • Plausible Analytics (aggregated analytics, if enabled)

No other third-party processing takes place.

International Data Transfers

Depending on the infrastructure provider, technical data may be processed outside the European Economic Area.

Such transfers are subject to appropriate safeguards under applicable data protection laws.

User Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request rectification or erasure
  • Object to processing
  • Lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

As this service does not store personal data, these rights may be limited in practice.

Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption
  • In-memory processing
  • Restricted logging
  • Rate limiting and abuse prevention
